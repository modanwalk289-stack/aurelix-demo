Our Story

Born from
obsession.

In 2009, founder Alexandre Levi spent three years wandering the souks of Marrakech, the flower markets of Grasse, and the oud distilleries of Oman — not buying perfume, but understanding scent as language.

What emerged was AURELIX: a house that refuses to separate artistry from identity. Each bottle is a statement. Each formula is a philosophy. We do not make perfume for the occasion. We make it for the person you become when you wear it.
